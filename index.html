Frequently asked questions

I used your app with my Github username and it didn't show anything on the chart. Why ?

The developers that built this app are not really good in Javascript. This may be a known bug that wasn't resolve. To get your stats, you should click on the update button.

I just clicked on the update button and still nothing. This app is really bad. Why can't I get my stats ?

This app only take the repositories that are on your Github page, so if you have committed on other repositories, your stats won't be updated. If you don't have any stats, that means that you have no eligible repository on your personal Github page.

I just created a Github account and I committed 14 times in 7m53s in a python repository that is ON MY PERSONAL GITHUB PAGE.
I should have a frequency of 2557,293868921776 commits/days and I should be first on the leader board. Why I don't have any stats on my account ??????

To prevent from cheater and to preserve good statistics, only the repositories where your first and last commits are separated by at least 1 days are considered.
